  • Implemented AI agents with human-like behaviour profiles to simulate and measure difficulty

  • Research published in IEEE scientific journal

Personal · 2020 · UE4 · Python / TensorFlow
Neuroevolution Driving
🧬
Overview

A personal project combining Unreal Engine 4 and Neural Networks. Starting from UE4's VehicleAdvanced template and a TensorFlow plugin, I trained a neural network with neuroevolutionary techniques to drive a vehicle around a full track without crashing. Agents trained on complex tracks generalised correctly to unseen irregular tracks.

Technical Details
  • UE4 VehicleAdvanced template as base environment
  • TensorFlow (Python) integrated via UE4 plugin
  • Neuroevolutionary training: population selection, mutation and fitness evaluation
  • Tested best models on unseen irregular tracks to validate generalisation
  • Compared models trained on simple vs complex tracks to analyse overfitting

Personal · 2020 · Unreal Engine 4
DailyLife Simulator
🏠
Overview

A personal project to master Unreal Engine's Behaviour Trees and Environment Query System (EQS). NPCs navigate a house and perform a realistic daily routine autonomously, choosing from a variety of solo and cooperative activities, then going to sleep at night.

Technical Details
  • Behaviour Trees driving all NPC decision-making and activity scheduling
  • Environment Query System (EQS) for spatial awareness and activity location selection
  • Solo activities: Idle, Sitting, Reading, Smoking, Drinking Coffee, Playing on a console
  • Cooperative activities: Conversation, Ping Pong, Playing on a console together
  • Night cycle: NPCs stop all activities and autonomously go to sleep
